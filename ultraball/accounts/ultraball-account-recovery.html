ULTRABALL ACCOUNT RECOVERY

Did you lose access to you account that is connected to an email? These are the instructions to help you get back onto the pitch.

If you happen to have forgotten what email you used, then follow these instructions:

  • Search through your email accounts for any emails mentioning an ULTRABALL account. This will help you narrow down which email you may have used.
  • Once you have found the email try logging in to your ULTRABALL account in-game.
  • You may have to click "Forgot Password" if you don't remember what password you used.
