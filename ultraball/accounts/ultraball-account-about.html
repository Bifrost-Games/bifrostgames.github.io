ABOUT ULTRABALL ACCOUNTS

ULTRABALL accounts is a custom account service that saves all your game data to our servers so they can be accessed on any device you login to.  

When you load into the game for the first time you will be prompted to login or register for an ULTRABALL account. Simply fill out the form with your email, username, and password. Then bam! You have yourself an ULTRABALL account!

Also, to get playing faster, click the "Remember Me" button when logging in or registering. And at anypoint you want to logout, simply click the "Log Out" button located top right on the Main Menu.

All your game data such as settings, inventory, and rank will be saved on our servers so you don't have to remember your settings when you log into your account on a different computer!
